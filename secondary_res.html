Farmers Markets Positive Effects on Low-income Communities and during Crisis

Article 1: “Farmers' market gets busy after grocery stores cleared out”
This article discusses how farmers markets have saved the day during the weather crisis in Texas. With grocery stores closed down and vast power outages, many have turned to farmers markets to provide food in this time of crisis, and vendors have stepped up to the challenge.

Article 2: “The seeds of a mobile Black farmers market are sprouting”
This article describes a new organization that brings a farmers market food truck sponsored by black-ownd businesses and farmers to low-income areas of Los Angeles, providing food for and educating the local community on nutrition.

Article 3: “Climate Corps America: The urban farms transforming how America’s most vulnerable communities eat”
This article goes into more detail on the benefits of local eating and farmers markets reaching more vulnerable communities, and how this improves the lives of the americans and the climate.d

Similar Websites to “The Bulb” that have effective designs:

Food Share
This website has a minimalist layout without too many distracting elements, a clear and readable map that the user can interact with, and a simple yet effective hierarchy, with navigation buttons and CTA’s that are useful to the user.

Forest Grove Farmers Market
This website utilizes photos well, showcasing the products and community, and is pretty easy for the user to navigate throughout. They could however, use some more unique design elements to capture the users interest.
