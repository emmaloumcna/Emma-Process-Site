Design Challenge

What's the problem you're trying to solve?

AProviding affordable and healthy food options for low income areas, while also supporting local farmers


The Design Question

How might we attract donors, farmers, volunteers, and establish ourselves as a part of the Charlotte community?


The Ultimate Impact

We want to improve the lives of low-income families and connect local communities


Possible Solutions

CTAs for farmers, volunteers, and donors. Easy to access information and contact numbers. Easy to understand locations and schedules. Mobile friendly


Context and Constraints

The organization is volunteer based. COVID creates limitations. Local farmers need enough revenue to continue partnering. Low-income families may have less internet access.


The Design Question (Restated)

How might we connect low-income families with local farmers through affordable farmers markets and wellness education?
